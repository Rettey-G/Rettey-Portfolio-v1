F&B Outlets Story – Where Culinary Meets Adventure

Immersive Storytelling Brand Narrative Concept Development

The Story Behind the Story

Ever wondered how a shipwrecked artist's survival tale could inspire an entire resort's dining experience? F&B Outlets Story reimagines restaurant menus as chapters in the life of "Seb," an artistic castaway whose ingenuity transforms island struggles into unforgettable culinary adventures.

From crafting rum from sugarcane (Seb's Shack) to building watchtowers for rescue (Watch Tower Dinner), each outlet's theme ties Seb's journey to a unique guest experience—blending storytelling with operational savvy.

Hook + Functionality

Playful lore (e.g., lanterns made from driftwood at Mizaj) paired with practical details (opening hours, specialties).

Brand Magic

Aligns with Raaya by Atmosphere's ethos of discovery, turning menus into conversation starters.

Expandable Universe

Ready for social spin-offs ("Survival Tips from Seb") or interactive guest activities (Farm to Table gardening workshops).
